Child Safety Standards

MyGGV Mobile Application

Last updated: January 2026

1. Our Commitment

MyGGV is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, including the protection of children. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any form of child exploitation on our platform.

2. Age Requirement

MyGGV is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account and use our services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

3. Content Moderation

We actively work to prevent and remove content that exploits or endangers children:

  • User-generated content is subject to review and moderation
  • We use a combination of automated tools and human review to detect prohibited content
  • Accounts that violate our child safety policies are immediately suspended and permanently banned

4. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns through the following channels:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the report feature available on any content or user profile within the app
  • Email: Contact us directly at support@myggv.com

All reports related to child safety are treated with the highest priority and reviewed promptly.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with all applicable laws regarding child safety. When we become aware of apparent child exploitation:

  • We report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) as required by law
  • We cooperate with local and international law enforcement agencies
  • We preserve relevant evidence as required by applicable legal obligations

6. Prevention Measures

We implement the following measures to protect minors:

  • Age verification during account registration
  • Privacy settings that limit exposure of user profiles
  • Restrictions on direct messaging features for younger users
  • Regular review and updates of our safety policies and tools

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our child safety standards or wish to report a concern, please contact us at:

Email: support@myggv.com
